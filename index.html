⚖️ Important Rules & Rights for Women
1

Equal Remuneration Act, 1976

Mandates equal pay for equal work regardless of gender. Employers cannot pay women less than men for the same or similar work.

2

Maternity Benefit Act, 1961 (Amended 2017)

Entitles women to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for first two children. Companies with 50+ employees must provide crèche facilities.

3

POSH Act — Prevention of Sexual Harassment, 2013

Every organization with 10+ employees must have an Internal Complaints Committee. Women can report within 3 months of an incident.

4

33% Reservation in Government Jobs

Horizontal reservation for women in central government Group C & D posts. Several states offer up to 50% in police and teaching roles.

5

Right to Safe Working Hours

Under the Factories Act, women cannot be made to work before 6 AM or after 7 PM without consent and adequate safety arrangements.

6

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme

Central government scheme to promote welfare and education of the girl child with financial incentives and awareness programs across all districts.

7

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Government savings scheme for girl children below 10 years. Offers 8.2% annual interest, tax-free returns, matures when girl turns 21.

8

Right to Free Legal Aid

All women are entitled to free legal aid regardless of income under the Legal Services Authorities Act. Contact your District DLSA for help.
